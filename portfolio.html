Get Ready:
An automatic closet which choses your clothes for you that I coded the entirety of. It has the functionality to: choose a random outfit for you with items of clothing that all go together, to chose a random outfit that includes preset items of clothing with items that all go together, instant feedback on what items go with what you've picked so far based on what you have taken from the closet, and more.
Automatic Modern Art:
An app that makes modern art for you automatically, with with a range of settings that allow in depth control over how your art will come out.
The Amazing Stopwatch:
A stopwatch with the functionality to pause, restart, lap, split, and can even remember a time that you can then recall later, along with that time's laps and splits. The recalled time can be resumed so you can start the timer from a saved time.
